WHO
WE ARE

PT Pison Mulia Perkasa (PMP) was founded in 2023. We are engaged in the supply and distribution of replacement spare parts for earthmoving equipment including: Undercarriage, G.E.T., Filters and other related components.

We provide products of the best quality, so that they can meet the usage standards for each equipment. Therefore, we hope to be able to reach the needs of national and international markets.

  • Undercarriage Parts — Track Shoes, Rollers, Idlers, Sprockets
  • G.E.T. — Ground Engaging Tools for all major brands
  • Filters — Air, Fuel, Oil & Hydraulic across all makes
  • Products meeting equipment usage standards
  • Serving national and international markets
Industry Sectors
INDUSTRIES
WE SERVE

Our products cover heavy equipment spare part needs across multiple extractive and heavy construction industries.

MINING
Coal, nickel, gold, tin. Spare parts for excavators, dump trucks, dozers, and graders at open-pit and underground mine sites.
CONSTRUCTION
Large-scale infrastructure projects. We supply components for cranes, compactors, and the full fleet of heavy construction equipment.
PLANTATION
Palm oil and forestry operations. Components for bulldozers, motor graders, and land clearing equipment always available.
ENERGY
Oil, gas and power generation projects. Spare parts for drilling, handling, and heavy transport equipment in energy operations.
